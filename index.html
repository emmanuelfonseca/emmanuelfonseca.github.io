Emmanuel Fonseca

Hello / hola / salut! I currently work as a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Physics at McGill University, and as part of the McGill Space Institute. I will be joining the Department of Physics and Astronomy at West Virginia University (WVU) in Fall 2021 as an assistant professor.

My area of research is radio-transient astronomy, with particular focus on radio pulsars and fast radio bursts. I am a proud member of the radio-astronomy group led by Dr. Victoria Kaspi, and was previously a part of of the group led by Dr. Ingrid Stairs during my graduate studies.
